M(L)ove — dance pose comparison

Two clips of the same routine play side by side. MediaPipe Pose (the real Tasks-Vision PoseLandmarker) tracks 33 body keypoints on each, frame by frame, and a limb-direction cosine similarity scores how closely the two dancers' bodies match in real time.

⚠️ Simplified demo. Pose detection is genuinely MediaPipe running in your browser; the similarity score is a lightweight limb-angle comparison, not a full choreography-grading model. It downloads the pose model (~5 MB) on first run.
