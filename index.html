Dance, graded
frame by frame.

Video can teach you choreography — but it can't tell you your arm angle is off by 20°. M(L)ove compares your attempt against a reference: pose estimation extracts 17 keypoints per frame, cosine similarity scores every frame, and a per-joint breakdown shows exactly where you diverge.

how it scores
Pose in, similarity out.
🦴

17 keypoints / frame

Each frame runs through a pose model (COCO schema), mean-centered and L2-normalized so scoring is invariant to position and body scale.

📐

Cosine similarity

Per aligned frame-pair, the cosine of the two normalized pose vectors yields a 0–1 match; the average is your grade.

🎯

Per-joint breakdown

Per-joint Euclidean distance flags which body part diverges most — left wrist? footwork? — making the feedback actionable.
